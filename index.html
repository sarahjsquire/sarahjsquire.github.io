Sarah Squire

Visual journalist, designer, and web developer.

I am a graphics developer at MarketWatch in New York City. Leading up to this, I was the summer 2012 online graphics intern for The Wall Street Journal, online editor (2011) and design editor (2010) at The Michigan Daily, and one of the editors and lead designer of Michigan Football: A History of the Nation's Winningest Program. I graduated from the University of Michigan with a B.S. and a concentration in a self-designed curriculum, Digital & Visual Media Studies.
