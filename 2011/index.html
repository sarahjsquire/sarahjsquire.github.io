Sarah Squire

Visual journalist, developer and designer.

I am the Visual Editor for Features at The Wall Street Journal in New York City. Other than that, this website hasn't been updated since 2013.

Leading up to this, I worked on the WSJ interactives team and made interactives and dabbled in data and reporting at MarketWatch. I was the summer 2012 online graphics intern for The Wall Street Journal, online editor (2011) and design editor (2010) at The Michigan Daily, and one of the editors and lead designer of Michigan Football: A History of the Nation's Winningest Program. I graduated from the University of Michigan with a B.S. and a concentration in a self-designed curriculum, Digital & Visual Media Studies.
